Requirements

The following are to be submitted along with the completed application form:

1.Four photographs of student (one attached to application form)

2.One photograph of mother

3.One photograph of father

4.Attested copy of birth certificate of student clearly specifying date of birth (issued by Municipal Corporation or any other competent Authority)

5.Transfer certificate (if applicable) from previous school.

6.Report/mark sheet (if applicable) from previous school.
